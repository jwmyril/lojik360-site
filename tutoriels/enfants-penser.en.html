🕵️ Become a truth detective

Think clearly — Lesson 1: Don't believe everything!

Anyone can write anything on the internet. How do you know what's true? Follow the detective!

📺 Lea's story

Lea sees a video: “Drink this magic drink and you'll run faster than everyone!” Lea loves running… Should she believe it?

🪄 The 3 magic questions

Before believing something, a truth detective asks 3 questions:

1. 🧐 Who says it?

Someone who really knows… or someone who wants to sell you the drink?

2. 🔎 How do they know?

Is there real proof… or just a nice promise?

3. 🤔 Does it make sense?

Can a drink really make you faster than everyone in the world?

Think… then check with the 3 questions!
